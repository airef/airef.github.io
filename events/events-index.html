About AI Scripters Events

The AI Scripters community regularly hosts tournaments between AIs on different maps and settings. See the relevant links below. Most of these matches are streamed on our Twitch channel. Tournaments are often announced in our forum, though the Tournaments subforum requires an account to view it.

Additionally we discuss and announce the tournaments on our Discord server and Reddit. Discord is likely where you can find the most updated information, but this webpage will be updated frequently. During the downtime between tournaments we also maintain an AI Ladder.

Lastly, you can view our AI Tournaments History spreadsheet, which has the results of AI tournaments going back to 2016, with links to the tournament brackets and a ranking of AI results.
